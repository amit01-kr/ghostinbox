About Ghost-Inbox

Ghost-Inbox provides secure, anonymous, temporary email addresses that automatically self-destruct after 15 minutes. Our service is designed for privacy-conscious individuals who need to protect their primary email from spam, data mining, and potential security threats.

Unlike traditional email services that store your data indefinitely, Ghost-Inbox leaves no trace after the expiration period. All messages, account information, and metadata are completely wiped from our servers, ensuring your digital footprint remains minimal.

Our API-driven infrastructure ensures high reliability and security, with no personal information required to use the service. Simply generate an address, use it for your immediate needs, and let it vanish - leaving spammers and data collectors with nothing but a ghost.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ghost Inbox?

Ghost Inbox is a free temporary email service that lets you create disposable emails for OTPs, verification emails, and privacy protection.

How long does a Ghost Inbox email stay active?

Your temporary email stays active for the full countdown (usually 15 minutes). If the tab stays open, you can keep receiving emails until it expires.

Can I receive OTP and verification emails?

Yes, Ghost Inbox supports receiving OTPs, login codes, and verification links from most websites and apps.

Do I need to sign up or create an account?

No registration is required. A temporary email is automatically generated when you open the site.

Are the emails anonymous?

Yes. Ghost Inbox does not store personal data, making all email activity private and anonymous.

Can I use Ghost Inbox for Gmail / Instagram / Facebook OTP?

You can receive OTPs from most apps, but some major platforms may block temporary email domains.

What happens when the timer ends?

The inbox and emails are automatically deleted. You can generate a new address anytime.

Can I delete all emails?

Yes, you can delete individual messages or clear the entire inbox using the “Clear All” button.

Is Ghost Inbox free?

Yes, Ghost Inbox is completely free with no hidden charges.

Does Ghost Inbox help avoid spam?

Yes. Using disposable emails keeps your personal inbox spam-free and secure.

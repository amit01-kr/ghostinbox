About Ghost-Inbox

Ghost-Inbox provides secure, anonymous, temporary email addresses that automatically self-destruct after 15 minutes. Our service is designed for privacy-conscious individuals who need to protect their primary email from spam, data mining, and potential security threats.

Unlike traditional email services that store your data indefinitely, Ghost-Inbox leaves no trace after the expiration period. All messages, account information, and metadata are completely wiped from our servers, ensuring your digital footprint remains minimal.

Our API-driven infrastructure ensures high reliability and security, with no personal information required to use the service. Simply generate an address, use it for your immediate needs, and let it vanish - leaving spammers and data collectors with nothing but a ghost.
